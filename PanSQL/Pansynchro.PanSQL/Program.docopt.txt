Pansynchro PanSQL compiler.  Simply provide one or more PanSQL scripts to autogenerate your build pipeline.

Usage:
  PanSQL <script filename> ...
  PanSQL <script filename> --nobuild
  PanSQL (-h | --help)
  PanSQL --version

Options:
  -h --help     Show this screen.
  --version     Show version.
  --nobuild     Generate C# project(s) but do not compile them
